Introduction

Denote \(D^*\) and \(D\), the numbers of units that would fail before \(T^*\) and \(T\), respectively, and set

\[ \begin{align} S&=\sum_{i=1}^{D^*}X_{i,n}+(n-D^*)T^* \quad &D^* >1 \\ &=nT & D^*=0 \end{align} \] which is called total time on test. Thus, the hybrid censored data from exponential distribution.

Dicussion of \(\hat{\theta}\)

Lemma 2.1 (Chen and Bhattacharyya 1987) The conditional mgf of \(\hat{\theta}\), \(\phi_{\hat{\theta}}(w)\) is

\[ \begin{align} \phi_{\hat{\theta}}(w) &=(1-q^n)^{-1}\bigg(\sum_{d=1}^{r-1}\binom n d (1-\frac{w}{\theta})^{-d}\bigg) \end{align} \]
